# Privacy Policy for OPAD

**Last updated: February 5, 2026**

## Overview
OPAD ("One Product a Day") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
- **Ideas and content**: Your ideas, answers, and pitches are stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.
- **AI features**: When you use premium AI features, your idea content is sent to our secure servers to generate pitches and feedback. This data is not stored on our servers after processing.
- **No account required**: OPAD does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

## Data Storage
All your ideas are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your ideas unless you use the AI features, in which case the data is processed and immediately discarded.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple**: We use Apple's StoreKit for subscription management.
- **Anthropic**: AI features are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Data sent for AI processing is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.

## Your Rights
You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device.
